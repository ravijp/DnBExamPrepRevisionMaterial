## Model-answer pointers

- **Q1:** Rickets = physeal changes (cupping/fraying/splaying, widened physis) + bowing + rosary; osteomalacia = osteopenia + Looser zones (sites) + bowing.
- **Q2:** Subperiosteal resorption (radial middle phalanx = most specific), brown tumours, salt-and-pepper skull, rugger-jersey, acro-osteolysis, chondrocalcinosis, vascular calcification.
- **Q3:** Osteomalacia + 2° HPT + osteosclerosis + soft-tissue/vascular/periarticular calcification; amyloid (dialysis).
- **Q4:** Dual-energy attenuation → BMD; T-score cut-offs; Z-score use; pitfalls (degenerative sclerosis, aortic Ca, vertebral fracture falsely raise spine BMD) → QCT alternative.
- **Q5:** (a) 3 phases, cortical thickening, expansion, picture-frame vertebra, cotton-wool skull, sarcoma risk. (b) perpendicular cortical lucencies at classic sites = pseudofractures in osteomalacia.
